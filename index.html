About

Greetings, I am currently a PhD student at the University of Southampton, under the supervision of Corina Cirstea and Pavel Naumov. My PhD is now focussed on coalgebraic representations of two-player games. I generally like applying category category, and coalgebra theoery, to help us understand areas like automata, programming languages, game theory, machine learning, and logic, more deeply.

Before embarking on a PhD, I was a computer scientist at Netcraft in Bath for approximately 2 years, where we used various programming languages and web technologies for internet security. I have a BSc in Computer Science from Southampton University.
